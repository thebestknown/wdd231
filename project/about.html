About the Artist

Hello! I’m Jeimy Espinoza, a passionate digital artist and frontend developer. This project is a reflection of my love for art, technology, and interactive experiences.

I specialize in creating digital portraits using different styles like realism, cartoon, and sketch. Each piece tells a story, and through this gallery, I invite you to connect with them — and with me.

This website was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON as part of my WDD231 course. It’s not just a portfolio — it’s an experience designed for interaction, feedback, and inspiration.

Thank you for being here!
